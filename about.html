Our Hardware!

Our system helps farmers better manage their crops and make more informed decisions using in-field sensors and NASA satellite data. With this, we collect information about temperature, soil moisture, rain and airborne particles, which is combined with NASA data to give a more complete picture of what is happening in the area

Our Sofwtare!

The collected data is displayed on a simple and accessible panel, allowing the farmer to monitor everything that happens on his property in real time. This helps solve important problems such as efficiently using water to improve irrigation, preventing fires by monitoring hazardous conditions, detecting deforestation to protect areas, and predicting extreme weather events such as droughts and floods.
